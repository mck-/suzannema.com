Photo Exhibition - Vancouver

Sun Yat-Sen Classical Chinese Garden

From March 2 to March 30, 2015

In this intimate photo exhibition, journalist and author Suzanne Ma gives us a rare and exclusive behind-the-scenes look at the Chinese immigrant experience in Europe.

The photos, taken over the past five years, brings us to a town in eastern China with a remarkable 300-year history of migration to the West. From there, we follow the town's migrants on their journey to Europe's factories, kitchens and farms.

Book Reading - New York

Asian Research Institute - CUNY

Friday April 3, 2015, 6 p.m. EST

In this appearance, Suzanne will draw parallels between the Chinese experience in Italy and the Chinese-American experience. She will explore the "Made in Italy" myth: how Chinese immigrants are transforming Italy's fashion industry and talk about the fear and mistrust percolating in Europe as Chinese immigrant populations soar. Suzanne will also tackle some hard questions: Is life better in the West? And why do so many Chinese immigrants (the largest diaspora in the world) continue to seek better lives outside of China?
