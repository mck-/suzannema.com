March

Photo Exhibition - Vancouver

Sun Yat-Sen Classical Chinese Garden

From March 2 to March 30, 2015

In this intimate photo exhibition, journalist and author Suzanne Ma gives us a rare and exclusive behind-the-scenes look at the Chinese immigrant experience in Europe.

The photos, taken over the past five years, brings us to a town in eastern China with a remarkable 300-year history of migration to the West. From there, we follow the town's migrants on their journey to Europe's factories, kitchens and farms.
