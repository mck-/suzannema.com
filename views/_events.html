Meet Me In Venice Book Launch

Dr. Sun Yat-Sen Classical Chinese Garden

Saturday March 8, 2015, 2 p.m. PT

Dr. Sun Yat-Sen Classical Chinese Garden in Vancouver’s historic Chinatown

A party to celebrate the launch of Meet Me in Venice! Want to know what it takes to become a published author? Curious to know more about the Chinese in Italy?

Come listen to Suzanne speak about her amazing four-year journey tracking migrants from China to Italy, and be the first to see an exclusive exhibition of Suzanne’s photographs on Chinese immigrant life on display in the Hall of One Hundred Rivers.

Light refreshments will be served.

RSVP: suzanne@suzannema.com
